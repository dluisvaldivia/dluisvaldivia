## Projects

**[Bloom](http://147.93.114.210:3001/)** — B2B team platform
All-in-one workspace for specialist teams: chat, video, tasks,
calendar, and growth experimentation tools. I came in as the
UI/UX designer but ended up shaping the product requirements and
planning the build process too. React and Node.js.

**[That's Very ADHD](https://www.thatsveryadhd.com/)** — UX experiment
Built for an ADHD coach. The idea was to make the site feel like
an ADHD brain, not just describe one. Sluggish scrolling,
reminders triggered by mouse movement, text that shifts to
simulate dyslexia. The execution was interrupted but the concept
is sound and I am building a better version.

**[ExpenseVue](https://sample-service-name-ikx9.onrender.com/)** — Personal finance app
Built with three classmates during my full stack bootcamp.
Connected to a real banking API. The focus was on making
financial data readable for people who find numbers overwhelming.

**[YogaBuddy](https://dluisvaldivia.github.io/yogabuddy/)** — Kids yoga app
Built this for my own kids. Illustrated yoga poses with a hold
timer and audio cues that speed up progressively to keep them
engaged. Designed for young users which meant no instructions,
no text, just visuals and sound.
